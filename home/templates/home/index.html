About us

The Catholic Community of Technical University of Mombasa is a society of Catholics who came together to proclaim the gospel among themselves and to the outside world and enhance their spiritual, social, and economic growth in line with the teachings of the church.

OUR MISSION

To enhance spirituality and self- reliance of the Catholic Community and improve the quality of their lives by establishing structures, coordinating activities and events towards these ends.

OUR VISION

To improve unity, challenge and enable students, by the gospel of Christ and social teachings of the church, to live a spiritual, responsible, self-reliant and economically empowered life as responsible Christians and citizens.

OUR OBJECTIVES

  • To proclaim the word of God to people.
  • To nature members spiritually, morally, physically, socially, economically and intellectually.
  • To enlighten members on their roles in the society.
  • To help members realize and develop their talents positively.
  • Training leaders and enhancing responsibility.

OUR MOTTO

GROW IN CHRIST.
