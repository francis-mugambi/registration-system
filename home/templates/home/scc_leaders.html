SCC Leaders

Tum Catholic Community comprises of 8 (eight) Families with 3 (three) leaders per Jumuiya:

Chairperson

The chairperson is in charge of the SCC
Organizes and chairs all SCC meetings
Is a default member of the SCC committee headed by the SCC Coordinator
Makes necessary communications from the office to the SCC members

Project Manager

The project manager is in charge of all projects within the SCC
He/She is in charge of the finances of the SCC with conjunction with other members
Shall be a default member of the SCC committee headed by the SCC Coordinator

Secretary

Keeps all records of SCC meetings, activities and membership.
Writes minutes of the SCC meetings
Assumes the activities of the SCC Chairperson in his absentia.
Works hand in hand with the SCC Chairperson in ensuring proper communication
Shall be a default member of the SCC committee headed by the SCC Coordinator
